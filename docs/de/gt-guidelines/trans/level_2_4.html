Level 2

Umlauts are transcribed according to the original, i. e. the umlauts in the common forms Ä, Ö, Ü, ä, ö, ü are distinguished from those which are marked by a superscript e over a vowel. In the superscript e over a vowel, the graph is formed by a combination of two characters (e. g. u&#x0364;).
