Documentation of the OCR-D Structure Ground Truth

The OCR-D structure Ground Truth contains publications in which only the structures or regions were labelled. The individual regions are marked according to the PAGE scheme.

  • Definition
    The corpus contains only structural data. On the digital copy individual regions are labelled according to the PAGE scheme.
  • Background
    The OCR-D structure Ground Truth corpus contains publications from the period 1500 to 1900.
  • Overview and Concordance
    The overview and concordance shows in comparison the coding of zones and regions in DTA Zot format, DTABf (TEI) and PAGE format.
  • Structure concordance METS and PAGE
    METS maps the (logical) structure of a document. A logical structuring concerns individual chapters, paragraphs as well as special paragraphs (such as dedications, errata).
