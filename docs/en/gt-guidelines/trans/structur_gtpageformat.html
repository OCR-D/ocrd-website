Structure concordance to zones and regions in DTA Zot format as well as DTABf (TEI)

The overview and concordance shows in comparison the coding of zones and regions in DTA Zot format, DTABf (TEI) and PAGE format.

The overview and concordance compares encodings of zones and regions in the DTA Zot format, DTABf (TEI) and the PAGE format. The reference to elements in DTABf and PAGE refers only to basic elements and not their context. For this reason, reference should be made to the documentation of the DTABf and PAGE.
