Overview and Concordance

The overview and concordance shows in comparison the coding of zones and regions in DTA Zot format, DTABf (TEI) and PAGE format.

The reference to elements in DTABf and PAGE covers only basic elements and not their context. For more profound information, please refer to the DTABf and PAGE documentation.
