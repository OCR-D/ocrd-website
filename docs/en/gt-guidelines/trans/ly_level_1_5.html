Level 1

The minimum coverage of the Ground Truth includes:

  • TextRegion (Text region),
  • ImageRegion (Illustrations, photos),
  • GraphicRegion (book decorations, drawings),
  • SeparatorRegion (Dividing lines, separators),
  • Other
