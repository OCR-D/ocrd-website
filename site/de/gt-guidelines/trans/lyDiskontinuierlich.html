Floating Elements in the Print Space (floating)

It can happen that illustrations or explanations are not placed in the factually correct place where they are referenced, but interrupt the text in another place arbitrarily (for layout reasons).

Basically, such elements are hardly recognizable. However, if a clearly floating element is detected, it must be marked accordingly.
