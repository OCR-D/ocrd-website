Level 2

The maximum Ground Truth coverage includes all available regions and their sub-specifications:

  • TextRegion (Text region),
  • ImageRegion (Illustrations, photos),
  • GraphicRegion (book decoration, drawings),
  • SeparatorRegion (Dividing lines, separators),
  • TableRegion (Tables),
  • LineDrawingRegion, (Line drawings)
  • MathsRegion (Mathematical formulas),
  • ChemRegion (Chemical formulas),
  • MusicRegion (Notes),
  • AdvertRegion (Advertisement) and
  • NoiseRegion (Dirt, stains, noise)
  • Other
