Level 2

In level 2, the separator is viewed and marked according to its function. The separator clearly separates two parts from each other by different design elements (lines, dots, stars). If a vignette or specially designed decoration appears at the end of a page, it should be checkek whether it is an individually designed decoration or vignette. If it is individual, the book decoration is marked as Graphic.
