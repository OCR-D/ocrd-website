Distinction between I and J

The sets of black letter types usually have only one grapheme for the modern capital letters I and J.

  • Level 1
    In the transcription, this grapheme is represented according to its phonetic value as I or J graph.
  • Level 2 and 3
    The transcription is completely true to the original, including abbreviations.
